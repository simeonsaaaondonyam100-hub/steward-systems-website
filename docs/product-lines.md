# Product Lines

## Operavault

Operavault is an institutional and school operations platform.

### Audience

Schools, institutions, administrators, bursars, HR teams, academic leaders, department heads, admissions teams, procurement teams, and reporting stakeholders.

### Core Value Proposition

Operavault helps institutions coordinate departments, academics, HR, accounts, admissions, procurement, attendance, reports, workload, and appraisal workflows in one operational system.

### Website CTA

View the public Operavault demo, review the indicative pricing overview, or request a live Operavault demo.

### Backend Boundary

Operavault owns school and institution operational data, including tenant setup, departments, students, employees, academics, finance records, admissions, procurement, attendance, reports, workload, and appraisal data.

Steward Systems Core may store that a visitor is interested in Operavault or requested an Operavault demo. Core must not store Operavault tenant operational data.

The Steward Systems public website owns Operavault product positioning, the public-safe demo, pricing overview, request-demo funnel, and company-level business pipeline. The Operavault portal later owns tenant subscription plan, enabled modules, feature limits, billing/subscription status, module access enforcement, and internal plan configuration.

Public Operavault module, pricing, and roadmap claims should be checked against `docs/product-source-of-truth/operavault.md` before website copy is changed.

## Cantoria

Cantoria is a music notation, choir, and composition workflow product.

### Audience

Choirs, choir directors, music teachers, composers, arrangers, worship teams, ensembles, and rehearsal coordinators.

### Core Value Proposition

Cantoria supports music notation, solfa, SATB arrangement, choir rehearsal, composition, part extraction, playback, and exports so music teams can move from idea to rehearsal-ready material.

### Website CTA

Join Cantoria early access.

### Backend Boundary

Cantoria owns music projects, scores, solfa notation, SATB arrangements, audio, rehearsal files, compositions, part extraction, playback data, and exports.

Steward Systems Core may store that a visitor is interested in Cantoria or requested early access. Core must not store Cantoria project, score, audio, composition, or export data.

Public Cantoria claims should be checked against `docs/product-source-of-truth/cantoria.md` before website copy is changed.

## Steward Ledger

### Audience

### Core Value Proposition

Steward Ledger helps groups manage members, meetings, resolutions, treasury, investments, and approvals with clearer governance and financial accountability.

### Website CTA

Request a Steward Ledger walkthrough.

### Backend Boundary

Steward Systems Core may store that a visitor is interested in Steward Ledger or requested a walkthrough. Core must not store Steward Ledger treasury, meeting, resolution, member, investment, or approval records.
